> The browser software era is one of heterogeneous interfaces. Take a look at these screenshots from two of my favorite web applications: Figma and Linear ... though they have many of the same features ...they don’t share a single icon... — [Bring Back Idiomatic Design](https://essays.johnloeber.com/p/4-bring-back-idiomatic-design)

As a long-time Mac user, there are idioms I've internalized so deeply that *modern software* feels wrong. I prefer to use [Sketch](https://www.sketch.com) over [Figma](https://www.figma.com/), [Nova](https://nova.app) over [VS Code](https://code.visualstudio.com). But this feeling won't resonate with most people. OP calls it idiomatic design, I just want software to respect the platform its built for.
